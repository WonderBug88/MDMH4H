1. Open the hosted app and confirm the merchant lands on setup or results based on readiness.
2. Review setup readiness, required integrations, publishing settings, and legal links.
3. Open Results and confirm Route Authority renders merchant-facing publish and review workflow content.
4. Open Review and confirm rows moved out of Results appear in the dedicated queue.
5. Open Developer and confirm the Marketplace review checklist exposes callbacks, install state, and legal URLs.
6. Open Terms and confirm the public legal page resolves on the production host.
